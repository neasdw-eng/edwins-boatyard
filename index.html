What We Do

Expert Marine Services

From routine maintenance to emergency repairs, we deliver Bahamian craftsmanship trusted by boaters worldwide.

⚓

Haul-Out & Launch

Periodic haul-outs with our marine railway system. Careful handling for power and sail vessels up to 60 feet, with experienced crew on every lift.

🔧

Hull & Bottom Work

Complete hull maintenance including pressure washing, sanding, barrier coating, and anti-fouling paint. We restore your hull to peak performance.

🛡️

Long-Term Storage

Secure dry storage on Man-O-War Cay with regular monitoring. Hurricane prep and post-storm inspections included for peace of mind.

⚡

Emergency Repairs

When things go wrong on the water, we respond fast. Short-notice work handled with urgency — getting you back underway as quickly as possible.

🎨

Fiberglass & Gelcoat

Expert fiberglass repair and gelcoat restoration. From minor dings to major structural work, we bring your vessel back to showroom condition.

⚙️

Mechanical Systems

Engine service, prop work, shaft alignment, and through-hull maintenance. We keep the systems beneath the waterline running true.

Our Story

Built on Bahamian
Boat Building Heritage

Edwin's Boat Yard carries forward the proud tradition of Man-O-War Cay — an island legendary for its boat-building craft. We're a full-service facility where repeat customers come back year after year, trusting our team with their most prized possessions.

Whether you need a scheduled haul-out or an emergency repair at short notice, we handle every job with the same dedication to timely, efficient, quality work that has defined this yard for decades.
