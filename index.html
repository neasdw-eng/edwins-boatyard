The Yard

Boaters come back, year after year.

Edwin's Boat Yard is a full-service facility on Man-O-War Cay in The Abacos, Bahamas, serving power and sail boaters.

Most of our work is repeat business — owners returning season after season. When something needs doing on short notice, we handle it in a timely and efficient manner.

Services

What we do, and how we do it.

i.

Periodic Haul-Outs

Scheduled haul-outs for power and sail boaters maintaining their vessels through the season.

ii.

Long-Term Storage

Storage for boaters who keep their vessels with us between seasons or during longer periods away.

iii.

Emergency Repairs

When something needs immediate attention, we step in. Repairs handled with the urgency they require.

iv.

Short-Notice Work

Unscheduled jobs, taken on as needed, completed in a timely and efficient manner.
