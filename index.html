The Yard

Boaters come back, year after year.

Edwin's Boat Yard is a full-service facility on Man-O-War Cay in The Abacos, Bahamas, serving power and sail boaters.

Whether for periodic haul-outs or long-term storage, we pride ourselves on repeat business with customers coming back year after year. We also accommodate emergency repair and short-notice work in a timely and efficient manner.

Services

A full-service yard, under one roof.

i.

Haul-Outs & Storage

Periodic haul-outs and long-term storage for power and sail boaters — the core of what we do.

ii.

Hull & Paint

Sea Hawk Certified Applicator — featuring Sea Hawk Islands 44 antifouling. Bottom prep through topside finish.

iii.

Mechanical

Authorized Yanmar Dealer. Engine service, repowers, generators, and full mechanical work for power and sail.

iv.

Fiberglass & Carpentry

Fiberglass repairs, woodwork, painting and refinishing — structural rebuilds through cosmetic detail.

v.

Rigging & Canvas

Sails, sail repairs, rigging, splicing, canvas tops and covers — everything above the deck.

vi.

Crane Services

Stepping and unstepping masts, rigging outboards, lifting out engines and generators.

We also try to accommodate emergency repair and short-notice work in a timely and efficient manner.

The Abacos · Edwin's in Context

From the Airport

  1. Arrive in Marsh Harbour (MHH); clear immigration and customs.
  2. Step out to the taxi queue and ask for the Albury dock for Man-O-War Cay.
  3. Take the Albury's Ferry — the trip takes about 20 minutes.
  4. From the Man-O-War ferry dock, the office is a few blocks' walk.

By Water

Boaters with moorings can arrange to be dropped off at their vessel directly. Office coordinates are 26.596638° N, 77.007582° W — drop a pin and head in.

For ferry schedules and water-taxi service, see Albury's Ferry Service.

The Neighborhood

In the heart of Man-O-War Cay.

The yard is in the midst of stores, an ice cream parlor, and a very good restaurant on Man-O-War. House and boat rentals are nearby, and it's a short ferry-and-taxi ride to Marsh Harbour and the airport.
